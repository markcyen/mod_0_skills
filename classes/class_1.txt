class MathClass

Attributes
----------
teacher (string)
in_person (boolean)
current_number_of_students (integer)
absent (integer)
students (array)

Methods
-------
change_teacher (updates teacher for this MathClass)
go_virtual (sets in_person as false)
add_student (adds new student to students array)
count_students (updates current_number_of_students, to include additional student from students array, minus those who are absent)
